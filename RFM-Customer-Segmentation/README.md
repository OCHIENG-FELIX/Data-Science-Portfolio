# RFM Customer Segmentation Analysis

**Customer Analytics Project**

Performed Recency, Frequency, and Monetary (RFM) analysis on the Superstore retail dataset to segment customers into meaningful business groups.

### Objectives
- Identify high-value customers
- Detect at-risk and lost customers
- Provide actionable insights for customer retention and marketing strategies

### Tools
- Python, Pandas, Plotly
