# Sales Trend & Seasonality Analysis

**Time Series & Business Trend Analysis**

Analyzed 4 years of Superstore sales data (2014–2017) to identify trends, growth patterns, and seasonal behavior.

### Key Analysis
- Yearly sales growth trends
- Monthly and quarterly seasonality patterns
- Peak performance months (especially November and December)
- Relationship between sales and profit over time

### Tools
- Python, Pandas, Plotly
